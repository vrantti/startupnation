What is Kasvuhuoneilmiö?

Kasvuhuoneilmiö (Greenhouse Effect) is our flagship green tech initiative dedicated to nurturing startups that are building solutions for climate change and environmental sustainability. We believe that technology and innovation are the keys to solving our planet's greatest challenges.

Our Mission

To accelerate the development of climate tech startups by providing them with the resources, mentorship, and funding needed to scale their impact and create a more sustainable future for all.

Focus Areas

  • Renewable Energy Solutions
  • Circular Economy Technologies
  • Clean Transportation
  • Carbon Capture & Storage
