HELLO DEAR POSSIBLE CREATIVE REVOLUTIONIST!

We'd like to invite you to be part of Demo Reboot, a next-level creative weekend happening 24.10.2025 1600 - 26.10.2025 1200 at Tampere Nokia Arena - Paidia. This event is organized as a non-profit initiative via Tampere Startup Association, with one mission: reboot the Finnish demoscene and give creators, coders, artists, and musicians a place to jam, collide, and spark new ideas.

WHY SUPPORT?

By sponsoring Demo Reboot, you're not funding a traditional event. You're fueling:

  • Innovation Access → fresh demos, bold experiments, prototypes, and art styles.
  • Outside-the-box thinking → non-conventional responses and unpredictable creative sparks.
  • New Talent Discovery → connect with exceptional developers and artists who rarely fit into "official" spaces.
  • Cross-sector Inspiration → games, art, tech, museums, culture — all colliding.
  • Movement & Identity → be part of the Renaissance of Attitude — a grassroots cultural reboot, not a KPI-driven program.

HOW IT WORKS:

• 50% of sponsorship goes directly to the creators.

• 50% builds the permanent DEMO HOUSE in Tampere — ensuring the spark lasts beyond the weekend.

As a sponsor, you can drop challenges, test ideas, or request creative input — and see what the community produces in real time.

"This isn't about polished outcomes. It's about energy, experiments, and raw creativity — exactly where innovation comes from."

We'd love to have you join as a sponsor and stand with us in shaping something bold, authentic, and future-facing.
