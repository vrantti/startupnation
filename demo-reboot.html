DEMO REBOOT

Tampere Nokia Arena - Paidia • 24.10.2025 1600 - 26.10.2025 1200 • The Renaissance of Attitude

NO RULES. NO KPIS. JUST CREATION.

Sign up to book a creator place in the venue to develop for two days your own projects and get to see what other crazy creators are working on. We also hack some challenges and have our speakers share their insights and play games, stream gaming and have side action to keep things in motion. You can join as visitor without registeration.
